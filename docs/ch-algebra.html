### How does R handle mathematically invalid operations?
### Example 1:
w = c(1:3)  # short-handed for c(1,2,3)
### Q: What is the length of w?

x + w  # no error displayed, but not quite a mathematically valid operation
### Q: what did R just do???
c(x[1:3]+w, x[4:6]+w)
